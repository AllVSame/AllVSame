About AllVSame

AllVSame helps you make smarter shopping choices by comparing the ingredients of brand-name products against cheaper supermarket alternatives.

Simply scan a product's barcode, and we'll find matching products from Tesco, Sainsbury's, Asda, and Morrisons that share the same or similar ingredients — often at a fraction of the price.

How It Works

  1. Scan a product barcode using your camera
  2. We look up the ingredients in our database
  3. We find alternatives with matching ingredients
  4. Compare prices and save money!

Data sourced from Supabase & Apify. Always check product labels for the most accurate ingredient information.
